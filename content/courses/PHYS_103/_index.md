## Course Description

Topics include basic concepts of motion, forces, work-energy, heat, electricity, magnetism, light and matter. Easy to understand algebra-based mathematics will be used. Those who are interested in learning the rules of nature are welcome to join. Throughout the semester there will be interactive lectures supported by videos, demonstrations and, along with laboratory observation.

## Main Course Objectives

* **Learn about the fundamental laws and principles of Physics that changed the world**
* **Understanding Physical phenomenon with scientific approach.**
* **Develop scientific reasoning skills.**
* **Analyze, and interpret data from scientific experiments.**
