## Course Description

This course will provide solid introduction to fundamentals of classical and statistical thermodynamics and its applications. Including traditional introductions to laws of thermodynamics, work –Heat and entropy. The latter half of the course dedicated to statistical interpretations of systems.

* Introduction to thermodynamic systems, properties and processes.
* Zeroth law of Thermodynamics and Temperature
* 1st Law of thermodynamics, Heat Engine and Carnot Cycle.
* 2nd law of thermodynamics Carnot theorem, entropy
* Thermodynamic Potentials, chemical potential
* 3rd law of thermodynamics and cooling.
* Kinetic theory of Gas, Fundamentals of Statistical mechanics.
* Heat capacity of solids and gases*
* Bose-Einstein, Fermi- Dirac gases 
## Main Course Objectives

* **Learn about the fundamental laws and principles of thermodynamics**
* **Understanding thermodynamic systems and their applications in real world.**
* **Statistical interpretations on microscopic and macroscopic properties of system**
